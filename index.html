Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?
Reservations are recommended, especially for evenings and weekends. Walk-ins are welcome when space is available.
How do I change or cancel?
Reply to your confirmation email (once the backend is live) or call the restaurant as early as possible so we can accommodate others.
How many guests can I book for?
You can request any party size. For larger groups, we may follow up to confirm seating and timing.
Can I add dietary notes?
Yes—use the “Special Request” field for allergies, accessibility needs, or any special occasion notes.
